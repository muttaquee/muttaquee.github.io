# Welcome — why I'm writing weekly

I've decided to share one short post every week. Partly to think out loud, partly to keep a public trail of the PhD as it happens.

## What this blog is about

My research sits at the intersection of **AI, virtual reality, and human–computer interaction** — specifically, how *AI-driven MetaHumans* (photorealistic, real-time virtual humans) can deepen immersive brand engagement.

Expect posts on:

- Building and driving MetaHumans in Unreal Engine
- Conversational and generative AI for virtual humans
- What the literature says about presence, trust, and engagement
- Honest notes on what's working — and what's breaking

## Why weekly

Small and consistent beats big and rare. A weekly cadence keeps the ideas moving and gives anyone following along a reason to come back.
