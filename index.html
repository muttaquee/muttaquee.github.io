01 — About

My journey

I'm a final-year PhD researcher at Teesside University, working where artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and human–computer interaction meet. My thesis explores how AI-driven MetaHumans — photorealistic, real-time virtual humans built in Unreal Engine — can deepen immersive brand engagement.

I combine real-time character technology, conversational and generative AI, and rigorous user studies to understand how virtual humans shape attention, trust, and emotional response in immersive brand experiences.
